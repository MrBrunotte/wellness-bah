Lifestyle


A peak into your future lifestyle!


I have a great lifestyle if you ask me! I live in a country with lots of sun and warm weather. I havent regreted for a moment the time when I decided to change my life and go vegan and base my health on the plant based way of eating. I used to eat dairy and a little bit of meat but after cutting this out my life has turned to the better both healtwise and even mentally.
A typical day in my life starts off early in the morning with a balanced breakfast, I then do my morning yoga routine. I try to do this every morning. After my yoga session I attend to my work. For lunch I eat the food that I prepared at last nights dinner, I try to always cook my dinner so that I will have lunch the day after. By doing this I free up time at my lunch break the next day.
After lunch I usually do some more work and I always make sure to walk my two dogs on the beach near my house. This walk is both for me and my dogs, we all love it and get some additional exercise.
I always cook my dinner and to me this is a time that i really enyou, I have some time for myself and my thoughts, which is an important time for me.
After dinner I usally visit my daughters or spend some time with friends.
My evening end around 10:30pm to make sure that i get enough sleep.
